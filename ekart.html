About The Challenge

The Edison Motors's Highschool EV Challenge is a competition with a mission to promote a passion in EV technology among today's youth. Students are tasked to build an E-Kart with the ability to achieve record speeds, pull heavy loads, and wind through on rough terrain. For more information, please check the competition website at https://www.edisonmotors.ca/highschool-ev-challenge. As part of the challenge, students will have the opportunity to practice essential skills in the workforce.
